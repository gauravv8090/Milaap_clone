What are payment gateway charges and why is it charged?

Whenever you purchase (eg book tickets) or make an online payment, it needs to be securely processed and verified by a third party payment processor.

  1. These processors include Banks (for netbanking, IMPS, NEFT, RTGS transactions), Visa, Mastercard, AMEX, Rupay etc (for credit and debit card transactions), Paytm, UPI apps and other payment wallets who charge a small fee for this service (‘payment gateway charges’). These charges on crowdfunding websites are unavoidable.
  2. This is NOT Milaap fees. These fees go directly to the payment processor and help us keep Milaap a safe place to donate. Payment gateways charge their fees as a % of donation amount made. It varies based on the mode of payment and service provider.

For donations made in Indian Rupees (INR)

UPI apps - 0.75% to 2.45%

Paytm - 1.92%

Debit/Credit cards - 2.33%

Netbanking - 2.51%

Amazon Pay - 2.71%

For donations made in US Dollars using a card issued in the USA (USD): 2.9% + 30cents per donation

For donations made in other foreign currencies or in USD using a card issued outside the USA: 3.9% + 30cents per donation

Reference: https://stripe.com/us/pricing

Is the free fundraising applicable for organizations and not-for-profits?

Yes, Milaap charges NO platform fees for individuals, NGOs and organizations alike. In fact, organizations with 80G tax-exempt status also have the option to either issue tax-exemption receipts from Milaap's dashboard or issue it separately. Receipts to donors from Milaap's dashboard can be issued, at a click of a button. This feature is FREE to use and entails NO FEES.

Does Milaap help in outreach of campaigns?

Over 98% of Milaap's fundraisers which have raised money do it for FREE using our best-in-class social fundraising technology and round-the-clock assistance and support from our relationship managers.

In certain scenarios, where the beneficiaries come from a disadvantaged backgrounds with no digital literacy, exhausted most of their finances with no other sources available and require funds very urgently; Milaap may consider them on a case-by-case basis for outreach on social media and digital promotion platforms after verification and a signed request letter from the beneficiary. This is subject to availability of our team members, and situations where we believe promotions can achieve something significant for the cause.

Similar to payment gateway costs where it's paid to payment processor, outreach costs are paid towards social media and digital promotion platforms. To ensure that donor money goes primarily to the intended purpose, we cap the outreach costs to not more than 15% of funds raised. Please note that Milaap doesn't earn / keep any additional fee or commission for itself from the costs incurred through promotions.
